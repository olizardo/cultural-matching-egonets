\section{Highlights}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Pre-existing cultural tastes actively shape the survival of social ties. 
    \item Shared cultural interests and activities reliably stabilize social bonds. 
    \item Ignorance of a peer's cultural preferences independently accelerates tie decay. 
    \item Shared tastes protect strong ties, but weak ties decay despite cultural matches. 
\end{itemize}
